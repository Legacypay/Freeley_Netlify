Frequently Asked Questions

What is Freeley?
Freeley is a healthcare technology platform designed to streamline access to premium compounded medications. We connect you with independent telemedicine providers and 503A licensed compounding pharmacies to get personalized treatments delivered discreetly to your door.
Do you take insurance?
By cutting out insurance middlemen, we're able to offer faster care, greater transparent pricing, and specialized customized medication you couldn't otherwise get. We accept HSA and FSA cards.
Are the physicians real doctors?
Yes, our network exclusively consists of independent, board-certified medical providers licensed in your specific state. They review your medical intake to determine if the prescribed treatment is safe and appropriate for you.
How does shipping work?
Once your prescription is approved and compounded, it's sent via expedited shipping straight to your address in discreet, climate-controlled packaging. Free standard shipping is included in all memberships.
